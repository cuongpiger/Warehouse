VOV.VN - Prime Minister Nguyen Xuan Phuc on March 2 sent a message of congratulation to the new Prime Minister of Malaysia.Muhyiddin bin Muhammad Yassin was sworn in as the 8th Prime Minister of Malaysia on March 1.He also was once an important ally and held various positions in the administration of former Prime Minister Mahathir Mohamad, most recently as Interior Minister.
