VOV.VN - Prime Minister Nguyen Xuan Phuc on May 18 extended a message to congratulate Benjamin Netanyahu on taking office as Israeli Prime Minister.Also the same day, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Pham Binh Minh sent a congratulatory message to Israeli Foreign Minister Gaby Ashkenazi.

Vietnam and Israel established their diplomatic ties on July 12, 1993.
