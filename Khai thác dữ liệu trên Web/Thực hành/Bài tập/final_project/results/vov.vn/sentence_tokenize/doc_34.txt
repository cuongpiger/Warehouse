The 45MW Phuoc Ninh solar power plant was officially inaugurated in Thuan Nam district in southcentral Ninh Thuan province on June 22
Covering an area of 652ha it was invested by the Ninh Thuan Energy Industry a subsidiary of the TT Group at a cost of more than VND1 trillion US43 million
Construction began in March and was completed 15 days ahead of schedule despite the impact of COVID19 Chairman of the Board of Directors of Ninh Thuan Energy Industry Pham Cong Doan saidThe plant was connected to the national grid on June 10 and will generate an estimated 75 million kWh per year
Vice Chairman of the Ninh Thuan Provincial Peoples Committee Pham Van Hau said the inauguration of the plant marks a significant milestone for the province as it will contribute to local socialeconomic development and help ensure national energy securityThe TT Group is carrying out a number of other projects in clean energy including a US6 billion LNG project in southern Ba RiaVung Tau province another worth US44 billion in central Quang Tri province and a wastetoenergy plant in Hanoi
