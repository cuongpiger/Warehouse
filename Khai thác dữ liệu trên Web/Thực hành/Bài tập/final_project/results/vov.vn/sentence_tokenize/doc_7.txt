VOVVN Party General Secretary and President Nguyen Phu Trong on May 28 extended a message of congratulations to President of Myanmar Win Myint on the occasion of the 45th anniversary of bilateral diplomatic tiesThe same day Prime Minister Nguyen Xuan Phuc offered congratulations to State Counsellor of Myanmar Aung San Suu Kyi
Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Pham Binh Minh sent congratulatory messages to Foreign Minister Aung San Suu Kyi and Union Minister for International Cooperation Kyaw Tin on the occasion
Their messages applauded the tremendous achievements in bilateral ties over the past 45 years both within bilateral and ASEAN frameworks other subregional regional and international cooperation mechanisms
The two nations leaders expressed their confidence that with combined efforts of the two Governments and peoples the Vietnam Myanmar friendship and allaround cooperative partnership will grow further in the near future delivering practical benefits to the two peoples while making significant contributions to ASEAN Community for the sake of peace stability cooperation and prosperity in the region and the world
