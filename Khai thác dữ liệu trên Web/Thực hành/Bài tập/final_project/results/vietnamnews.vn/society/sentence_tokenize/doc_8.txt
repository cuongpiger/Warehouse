HÀ NỘI The Vietnamese and Japanese governments have agreed to gradually ease travel restrictions between the two countries the Ministry of Foreign Affairs has announcedRelated measures and procedures will be discussed via diplomatic channelsThe ministry said the two governments have worked closely in COVID19 prevention and controlThe Vietnamese Government praised the significant outcomes of Japanese efforts to curb the spread of the disease
On May 25 Japan lifted its state of emergencyThe Japanese Government meanwhile spoke highly of the Vietnamese Governments success in COVID19 prevention and control in particular reporting no new community infections for the last two monthsBoth governments have a shared view on the importance of bolstering their extensive strategic partnership in all fieldsSuch cooperation should be strengthened in a way that ensures effective COVID19 prevention and control so as not to cause the virus to spread and to fully comply with regulations in each country
