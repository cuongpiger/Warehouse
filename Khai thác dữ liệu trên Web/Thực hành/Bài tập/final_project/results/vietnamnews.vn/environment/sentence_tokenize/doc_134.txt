HÀ NỘI The Ministry of Nature Resources and Environment has urged relevant agencies to arrange activities during the Action Month for Environment in response to World Environment Day June 5Under the theme Time for Nature the agencies are also requested to bolster funding and mobilisation of social sources for nature and biodiversity conservationThey need to keep a closer watch on waste collection transport and treatment and address wasteinduced environmental pollution which directly hampers peoples livelihoods said the ministry adding that the building of environmentallyfriendly constructions for the sake of the public is encouraged
